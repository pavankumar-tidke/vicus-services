Our Vision

Providing Business Services by leveraging Technology, Automation, and Artificial Intelligence in a cost-effective way. Empowering our Clients through Business Transformation and our Remote Employees through a Decentralized Workspace.

Our Story

At Vicus Services, we believe there is a better way of handling your Non-Core Business Activities by using the power of AI and Automation to our advantage and synergizing it with empathetic Human Beings. We aim to help Start-Ups and Mid-Sized Businesses bring down their costs and provide employment opportunities throughout the world by sourcing the best talent as we are no longer restricted to geographical boundries.

Our Leadership

Harshith Vaddiparthy
Founder & Director

At times like these where we witnessed businesses collapse and unemployment rates spike, Harshith Vaddiparthy founded Vicus Services (OPC) Pvt. Ltd. to help other Businesses with their Non-Core Business Activities. Harshith's aim is to provide Business Services to Start-Ups and Mid Sized Businesses in a cost-effective way because he wants to help them with their Back-end. He also understands that every Start-Up has limited resources that can only be used in their Core-Business Activities to bring the best of what they have to offer to the world. And focusing on the Non-Core Business Activities can make it very difficult for a Start-Up to sustain in this competitive world.
